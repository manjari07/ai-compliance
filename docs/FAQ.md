# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### Q: What is AI Compliance?
A: It's ensuring your AI systems meet legal, regulatory, and ethical standards. Security, fairness, transparency, and accountability.

### Q: Do I need to use all of this?
A: No! Start with what matters for your situation. Add more as you go.

### Q: How long does this take?
A: Manual checklist: a few hours. Full implementation: 2-3 months. Ongoing: 4-8 hours/month.

### Q: What if I don't have a compliance team?
A: One person can do this. Might take 4-6 hours per week, but it's doable.

## Customization Questions

### Q: How do I customize this for my industry?
A: 
1. Read CUSTOMIZATION-EXAMPLES.md for your industry
2. Copy relevant sections into your policy
3. Add industry-specific checklist items
4. Update requirements

### Q: Can I remove controls I don't need?
A: Yes, but document why. Auditors will question missing controls.

## Checklist Questions

### Q: How often should I complete the checklist?
A: Minimum: Quarterly. Recommended: Monthly. Best: Weekly for high-risk systems.

### Q: How do I know if I'm "compliant"?
A: 
- 90-100%: Good - maintain
- 70-89%: Gaps - make a plan
- <70%: Critical gaps - fix immediately

### Q: What counts as "evidence"?
A: Screenshots, audit reports, test results, policy documents, audit logs, certifications.

## Implementation Questions

### Q: Which controls should I implement first?
A: **Critical (Week 1):**
- Access control
- Encryption
- Audit logging

**Important (Week 2-3):**
- Incident response
- System monitoring
- Authentication

### Q: What if I find gaps?
A: 
1. Don't panic - you caught it early
2. Prioritize by risk
3. Make a plan
4. Fix highest priority first
5. Check again in a week

## Code/Automation Questions

### Q: Do I need to implement the Python code?
A: No, it's optional. Manual checklist works fine for most teams.

### Q: When should I automate?
A: When you have:
- 3+ systems to track
- Want daily automated checks
- Have engineering resources
- Understand what you're checking

## Compliance & Audit Questions

### Q: Will this guarantee FedRAMP/compliance?
A: No. This helps you prepare and maintain compliance. Official audits are separate.

### Q: How do I prepare for an official audit?
A: 
1. Complete this template
2. Implement all required controls
3. Document evidence
4. Conduct internal audit
5. Engage official auditors

### Q: What if I find non-compliance?
A: 
1. Root cause analysis
2. Impact assessment
3. Immediate mitigation
4. Permanent fix
5. Process change to prevent recurrence
